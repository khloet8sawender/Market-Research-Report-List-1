<p><p>High power reed switches, rated above 30W, are designed for applications requiring reliable switching capabilities under higher loads. They come in two main market types: Normally Open (NO) and Normally Closed (NC). Normally Open reed switches remain off until activated by a magnetic field, making them suitable for triggering devices. Conversely, Normally Closed reed switches are activated to turn off in the presence of a magnetic field, ideal for safety and control applications. Both types are crucial in various industrial and consumer electronics sectors.</p></p>
