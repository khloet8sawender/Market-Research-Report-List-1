<p><strong>The PVA Glues Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVA glues, or polyvinyl acetate adhesives, are categorized into two primary market types: Type I and Type II. Type I glues are waterproof and suitable for exterior applications, making them ideal for outdoor projects or crafts exposed to moisture. In contrast, Type II glues are water-resistant but not fully waterproof, making them suitable for interior applications or projects that may encounter limited moisture exposure. Both types are favored for their versatility and ease of use in various bonding tasks.</p></p>
