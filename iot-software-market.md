<p><p>The IoT software market is primarily categorized into cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based IoT software leverages remote servers for data processing, storage, and analytics, offering scalability and easy accessibility via the internet. This model reduces infrastructure costs and enhances collaboration. In contrast, on-premises IoT software is hosted locally within an organization’s infrastructure, providing greater control, customization, and security.
