<p><p>The egg stabilizer market includes cream of tartar, sugar, and starch, which are vital for enhancing the texture and stability of egg-based products. Cream of tartar helps maintain the structure of whipped egg whites, preventing overwhipping and enhancing volume. Sugar stabilizes meringues and mousses by incorporating moisture and improving flavor. Starch, often used in various forms, thickens and stabilizes mixtures, providing improved shelf-life and texture in baked goods and confections. These stabilizers are essential in both commercial and home baking.</p></p>
