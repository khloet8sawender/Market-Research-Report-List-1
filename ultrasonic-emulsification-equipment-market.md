<p><p>Ultrasonic emulsification equipment facilitates the creation of stable emulsions through high-frequency sound waves. The Water-in-Oil-in-Water (W/O/W) type involves encapsulating water droplets within an oil phase, then further dispersing that mixture in water, ideal for controlled release applications. In contrast, Oil-in-Water-in-Oil (O/W/O) emulsions feature oil droplets dispersed in water and then suspended in a secondary oil phase, useful for precise ingredient delivery. Other types may include variations or specific applications tailored to unique industrial needs, enhancing formulation versatility.</p></p>
